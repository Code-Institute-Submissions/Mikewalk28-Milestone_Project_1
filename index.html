Nickelback

About:

Nickelback were formed in 1995 in Hanna, Alberta. The Band released its first album in 1996 named The Curb, however it wasn't until 2002 when they broke in to the mainstream with feature single How You Remind Me on album Silver Side Up.

Meet The Band:

Chad Kroeger

Born 1974, Chad is the lead singer and guitarist. He produces and song writes a lot of the bands work as well as collabing with many other bands such as Theory of a Deadman, Default and Saliva. He has featured on other artists songs such as Santana. Chad picked up the guitar at the young age of 13, however, he had a troubled youth and was sometimes in trouble; once even for breaking into his Junior High School, of which he wrote the song Photograph, one of their most popular records to date.

Ryan Peake

Born 1973, Ryan is the rhythm guitarist, keyboardist and backing vocalist. He has writing credits for many of Nickelback's songs but also performs lead vocals on cover songs the band performs, such as, Saturday Night's Alright For Fighting. Ryan grew up as a solid metal fan and credits them with for his musical tact and ability to harmonise.
